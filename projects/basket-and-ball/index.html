Basket and Ball

About This Game

Basket and Ball is an extremely fun and unique game that combined the elements of plaformer, shooting, and physics games! Not only do you have to control the ball over courses and obstacles and reach the hoop, you also need to shoot precisely and bouce the ball perfectly to score more stars! Jump higher by bouncing the ball many times and use the walls wisely to rebound the ball! There are even special Shootout Challenges where you have to collect stars while shooting into the basket in precision! There are 50 different fun levels to try, so what are you waiting for? Go shoot some baskets!

Controls

  • Left / Right Arrow Keys: Move
  • Space: Bouce
  • Drag: Aim
  • Drop: Shoot
  • Note: Only use Drag and Drop when at Shootout Challenges
