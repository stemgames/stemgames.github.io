Awesome Tanks 2

About This Game

Awesome Tanks 2 is a very popular shooting game where you control a tank to defeat enemies in different levels and difficulties and earn money! Move around and react fast to defeat enemy tanks and take cover so you don't get shot by them! You can also shoot explosives to kill enemies. Remember to defeat every enemy to finish the level and earn the maximum reward! Use the money to upgrade your tank in different ways. Have fun!

Controls

  • Arrow / WASD Keys: Move Around
  • Click: Shoot
  • Mouse: Aim
  • Number Keys: Switch Weapons
